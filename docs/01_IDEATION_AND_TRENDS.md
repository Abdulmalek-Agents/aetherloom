### 2.4 The Loom mechanic (Yusuf's psychology angle)

Hades is solo — the "narrative engine" is your one character's relationship with the cast. Aetherloom is 2-player — the "narrative engine" becomes the **relationship between the two siblings, mediated by the Loom**. Your choices weave threads that change *your sibling's next run*, even if they're not in your session. This creates:

1. **Asynchronous co-op pull** — "my sibling will see the boss I changed"
2. **In-relationship roleplay** — best-friends, siblings, partners, parent-child can each map onto the twins
3. **Streamer narrative moments** — "I wove this thread because my brother said…"

No other co-op roguelite has this layer.
