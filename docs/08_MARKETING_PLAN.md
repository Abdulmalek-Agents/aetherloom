# 📣 Marketing Plan — Aetherloom

> Audience: Hades fans + Children of Morta fans + Hi-Fi Rush fans + r/Roguelites. Multi-platform launch capitalises on a $1B-trajectory genre.

## 1. Target audience profile

### Primary: Action roguelite core
- 18–40 yo, plays Steam 5–20 hrs/week
- Owns Hades, Hades 2, Children of Morta, Wizard of Legend, Ravenswatch, Hauntii
- Watches Olexa, Retromation, Splattercatgaming, Wanderbots, TyrannicalRex
- Buys games $20–$30 day-one

### Secondary: Stylized 3D co-op
- 20–45 yo, plays with 1 specific friend/partner
- Owns Hi-Fi Rush, It Takes Two, Cult of the Lamb, Risk of Rain 2
- Wants narrative + combat depth in co-op (not party-game)
